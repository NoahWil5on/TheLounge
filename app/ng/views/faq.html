FAQ

What is At Your Service?

At Your Service is a mobile application created to provide a common platform for student to find services they’re looking for. This can include anything from a new roommate, repairing your bike or computer, finding housing, or even getting a ride.

Getting Started

Once you’ve created an account for At Your Service (using your college email) you will be able to explore services others have posted by searching for something you need to get done. These are just a few categories!

Rides



When you need to go somewhere

Fix It


When you need to fix something

Housing


When you need somewhere to live

Posting to At Your Service

Everyone has their own unique skills, and being in college people are always looking to get things done without hurting their wallets.

List


Look for something you need done and find the best people to get the job done.

Post

Respond


Send them a message.

I requested a service but I’m not satisfied or no show...
